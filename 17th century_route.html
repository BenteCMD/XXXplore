17th Century Route

Description

During the 17th century Amsterdam was the centre of world trade, art and the sciences. The city was in bloom and this wealth resulted in building the famous canal ring. During this route you'll get to know the beautiful Jordaan neighborhood. Cycle along the canalhouses, make a stop at the oldest bar in Amsterdam and come at peace in the courtyards.

Details

  • Duration:30 min.
  • Distance:4 km / 2.5 mi
  • Starting point:Karthuizersstraat 89-171
  • Amount of hotspots:5

Take this route during daytime for the best experience.
