Tips

  • Did you know there's a student-run organisation dedicated to helping international students? The International Student Network (ISN) organises introduction days, parties, weekend trips, excursions and other cultural activities. They have local Dutch coaches who guide international students during the introduction period.
  • Every Saturday (9.00 am - 3.00 pm) and Monday (9.00 am - 1.00 pm) there is a market in the Jordaan. The Noordermarkt sells antiques, books, vintage clothing, jewelry and many various unique products!
